## Child Miners math quick note
- Designed at one-fifth payload per 70-day cycle to approximate annual goals (200 steel/oil, 100 alu/chrom/rubber, 50 tungsten).
- Because 70 days is slightly shorter than one-fifth of a year, perfect cycling gives a small overrun (~4%).
